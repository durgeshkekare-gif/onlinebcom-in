BCom subjects vs BBA subjects — key difference

BCom focuses heavily on: Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting, Business Law, Taxation (Income Tax + GST), Economics, Statistics, Corporate Finance, Auditing. Deep finance and legal foundation. BBA focuses on: Principles of Management, Marketing, Organisational Behaviour, Business Communication, Operations, Strategic Management, HR Management, Entrepreneurship. Broad management foundation.

Verdict — who should choose what

Choose BCom if: You want to become a CA, you're targeting banking/government competitive exams, you want a deep finance and accounting foundation, or you're cost-conscious and want the cheapest path to a valid commerce degree with the best government exam preparation.

Choose BBA if: You're clear about a management/marketing/HR/entrepreneurship career, you want broad business exposure, you're considering an MBA immediately after graduation, or you want to start a business and need management frameworks early.
