Amrita vs Manipal Online BCom 2026 — Which Is Better?

The two best online BCom programs in India — compared

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and Manipal University Jaipur are the two most recommended online BCom programs in India in 2026. Both are WES recognised. Both are UGC-DEB approved. Both are NAAC accredited. The differences are meaningful and the right choice depends on your priorities.

Amrita Online BCom — when it wins

Amrita Online BCom has NAAC A++ — the highest possible NAAC grade, held by fewer than 5% of Indian universities. It includes industry certifications from Oracle, NISM (NSE Institute), NSE and Amazon — which are bundled into the program at no extra cost. Fee: ~₹45,000/year (₹1.35L total). Best for: students who want the highest possible institutional accreditation and industry certifications that add immediate resume value.

Manipal MUJ Online BCom — when it wins

Manipal MUJ Online BCom has the lowest fee in this comparison — ₹80,000 total vs ₹1.35L for Amrita. Same WES recognition. NAAC A+ (one grade below Amrita's A++). No industry certs. Best for: students who need the lowest fee possible while getting a WES-recognised, NAAC A+ degree from a reputed private university.
