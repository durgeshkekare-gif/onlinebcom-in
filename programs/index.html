Frequently Asked Questions

Which online BCom is best in India 2026?

For overall value: Manipal MUJ (NAAC A+, WES, ₹80K — lowest fee for a WES-recognised BCom). For best accreditation: Amrita (NAAC A++, WES, Oracle+NISM certs). For budget: IGNOU (₹12–15K, valid degree). For Delhi brand: DU SOL. For Canada PR: Manipal MUJ or Amrita (WES recognised).

Is online BCom valid for CA registration?

Yes — ICAI accepts all UGC-DEB approved online BCom degrees for CA Foundation registration. This includes Manipal MUJ, Amrita, Amity, LPU, IGNOU, and DU SOL. The BCom provides the best subject preparation for CA exams — accounting, taxation, business law, and company law are BCom core subjects and CA exam subjects.

Can I do online BCom and work simultaneously?

Yes — online BCom has no attendance requirement. Study through a digital portal at your own schedule. All major universities (Manipal, Amrita, Amity, LPU) run online-only delivery — exams are either online-proctored or at authorised centres near you. Most students find 8–10 hours/week sufficient for online BCom coursework.
